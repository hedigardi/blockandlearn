# **App Name**: Block & Learn Landing

## Core Features:

- Brand Description: A concise introduction to Block & Learn, highlighting its mission to provide accessible education on Bitcoin, blockchain, and digital finance. Use the description provided from the YouTube Channel.
- Social Media Links: Display clear and direct links to Block & Learn's profiles on YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and X (Twitter).
- Contact Information: Provide an email or contact form for users to reach out with questions or collaboration requests.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Electric blue (#7DF9FF) to represent innovation and the digital space.
- Background color: Dark, desaturated blue-gray (#212A3E) for a modern, tech-focused feel.
- Accent color: Bright purple (#BE29EC) to highlight calls to action and key information, analogous to blue.
- Font pairing: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for headlines and 'Inter' (sans-serif) for body text to maintain a modern, readable aesthetic.
- Use simple, modern icons to represent each social media platform, ensuring they are visually consistent and easily recognizable.
- Implement a clean, single-page layout with a clear hierarchy, ensuring the brand description and social media links are immediately visible.
- Incorporate subtle animations on page load or hover effects on links to add a touch of interactivity and engagement.
